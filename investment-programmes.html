Advisor FAQ

How can I initiate an investment?
Direct equity investments (LSPP) are via invitation. Bond allocations (LDIB) are available during quarterly subscription windows through our tokenized platform.
What are the eligibility requirements?
Subject to KYC/AML regulations. Strategic Patrons (LSPP) are qualified institutional or high-net-worth investors. LDIB is open to global African Diaspora.
Risk disclosure & reporting?
We provide quarterly audit reports and real-time dashboard visibility for tokenized assets. Full risk disclosures are available in the Investor Deck.
