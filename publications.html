Africa Tokenization
Outlook 2025:
Digital Finance

A comprehensive deep-dive into the evolving landscape of asset tokenization across the African continent and its impact on cross-border liquidity.

Type Outlook Report
Release January 2025
Pages 48 Pages
Download PDF

Executive Summary

This report examines the rapid adoption of blockchain technology in the African financial sector. We analyze how tokenized real-world assets (RWAs) are creating new corridors for investment from the GCC region, providing unprecedented access to African growth opportunities.

Key Takeaways

  • 01 The rise of stablecoin-settled commodity trading in West Africa.
  • 02 Regulatory shifts in the EAC and SADC favoring digital asset custody.
  • 03 Case studies of successful tokenized debt-financing for SME growth.

Methodology

Our research team synthesized data from central banks, private equity firms, and on-chain analytics to provide a holistic view of the market's trajectory over the next 36 months.

Lead Researcher Dr. Amara Diop

Head of Research at Lumifrik Capital, specializing in emerging market financial architectures and digital asset regulation.
